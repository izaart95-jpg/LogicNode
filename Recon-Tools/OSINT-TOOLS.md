# Manual Phone Number Check (Instagram Method)

⚠️ This method only works ethically and legally.

If you want to check whether a phone number might be linked to an Instagram account:

1. Save the target phone number in your phone contacts.
2. Open Instagram.
3. Go to **Settings**.
4. Navigate to **Account → Contacts Syncing**.
5. Enable **Sync Contacts**.
6. Go to **Discover People / Add Friends**.
7. Instagram may suggest accounts linked to contacts in your phone.

> The target phone number must be saved in your contact list.
> Results are not guaranteed.
> Respect privacy laws and platform policies.
